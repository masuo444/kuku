About the Wooden Box (Masu) KUKU Carries

The "masu" that KUKU always carries is a traditional Japanese wooden vessel, symbolizing "giving form to thoughts."

The word "masu" contains the meaning of "increase" (to grow). A wish that gratitude, connections, and the power of creation will increase.

Through this masu, KUKU shares thoughts with friends around the world, symbolizing the "growing" of new culture together.

One person's thoughts "increase" to the world.
This is the beginning of KUKU's story.
