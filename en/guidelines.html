KUKU Creator Guidelines ver1.0 (Draft)

Version 1.0 (Draft)

Last Updated: October 19, 2025

KUKU is an open creative brand that can be freely used by creators worldwide. We welcome various forms of creation, including those utilizing AI. These guidelines define the rules for using KUKU characters, worldview, logos, etc. (hereinafter "KUKU Assets") in creation, distribution, and sales. Copyright belongs to FOMUS (※Not CC0).

#1. Basic Stance

  • Warmly Welcome: Fan works including fan art, doujin, videos, music, novels, games, and merchandise.
  • Non-commercial Use is Free: Free to use without permission with credit attribution.
  • Commercial Use Requires Application: We provide flexible permissions and conditions according to project scale.
  • Available to Creators Worldwide: We welcome AI-assisted creation. However, model training and redistribution require separate application.

#2. Usage Categories and Procedures

Category Examples Revenue Procedure
Non-commercial Portfolio, educational purposes, personal social media and videos without ad revenue $0 No permission required (credit mandatory)
Small-scale Commercial Doujin sales, small merchandise quantities, single videos on monetized channels, etc. Within $140,000 annual revenue Generally OK (credit mandatory, comply with terms). Consultation available for unclear cases
Medium to Large-scale Commercial Mass-produced merchandise, corporate projects, ongoing series, broadcast/streaming, in-game purchases, large-scale NFT issuance, etc. Over $140,000 annual revenue Permission required (prior application, contract, terms provided)

📌 Note: The guideline of "$140,000 annual revenue" refers to the annual sales of the specific business using KUKU. Feel free to consult us even during the planning stage.

#3. Prohibited Actions

  • Brand Damage: Expressions that significantly harm KUKU's image
  • Rights Infringement: Infringement (or risk thereof) of third-party copyrights, trademarks, portrait rights, etc.
  • Official Misrepresentation: Displays that mislead as official or collaborative works (including modification or misuse of official logos)
  • Unauthorized Secondary Use: Reusing, tracing, or reselling others' fan works
  • Inappropriate Content: Excessive sexual or grotesque content, discrimination, defamation, illegal or antisocial content, etc.
  • Logo Modification: Modifying KUKU logos or badges, creating confusing derivative logos
  • False Applications: False reporting of sales scale, intended use, etc.

#4. Platform-Specific Guidelines

UGC Game Items (e.g., Roblox, Fortnite, Decentraland, etc.)

Permission generally required due to similar item policies and review processes. Please consult before sales or distribution.

NFT

Small-scale, limited issuance is generally acceptable (credit required). Large-scale issuance or secondary market-focused requires permission.

⚠️ Warning: Trademark use as purchaser benefits is not permitted. Logo modification and official misrepresentation are prohibited.

Generative AI Model Training

Using KUKU Assets as training data or distributing models requires prior permission.

Publishing generated outputs is allowed (credit required / prohibited actions strictly enforced).

#6. Commercial Use Application (Over $140,000 annual revenue / Special purposes)

Please contact us with the following information. After review, we will provide contract terms.

  • Business content and purpose (medium / region / duration)
  • Scale (sales price, quantity, estimated annual revenue, KPIs)
  • Creative overview (samples acceptable)
  • Attribution plan (credit / logo placement)
  • Scheduled start date

Contact: For commercial use applications, please contact us via the application button below.

#7. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Can I use KUKU for school projects or personal social media?

→ Yes (non-commercial / credit required)

Q. Can I post KUKU videos on my monetized YouTube channel?

→ Generally yes (small-scale commercial). For series or sponsored content, please consult us.

Q. I want to distribute fan merchandise in small quantities.

→ Yes (small-scale commercial). Mass production or wholesale requires permission.

Q. I want to use it for a corporate campaign.

→ Permission required. Apply in advance.

Q. Can I change the logo colors?

→ No. Unmodified use only.

#8. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

In principle, UAE (Dubai) law serves as the governing law, with concurrent application of Japanese law available by mutual agreement when necessary.

#9. Changes to Guidelines

These guidelines may be revised according to operational circumstances or legal amendments. Please check the official website for the latest content.
