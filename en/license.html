Commercial License Application

For those planning to use KUKU materials commercially, please apply through this page.
Small-scale commercial use with annual revenue under $140,000 generally requires no permission.

üìã License Overview

KUKU is an open IP that allows creators worldwide to create freely. Non-commercial use is free, but commercial use requires application depending on the scale.

Usage Categories

  • Non-Commercial Use: Portfolio, educational purposes, personal social media and videos (non-monetized) require no permission (credit required)
  • Small-Scale Commercial Use (Annual Revenue under $140,000): Doujin sales, small-scale merchandise, one-off monetized videos, etc. Generally permitted (credit required)
  • Medium to Large-Scale Commercial Use (Annual Revenue over $140,000): Mass-produced merchandise, corporate projects, ongoing series, broadcasting/streaming, in-game purchases, etc. Requires permission (prior application required)

üìå Important: "Annual revenue of $140,000" refers to the annual sales of the business using KUKU. Feel free to consult even at the planning stage.

The following information is required for commercial use application.
