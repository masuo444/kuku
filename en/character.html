COMPANIONS

Companions

Unique companions who protect the forest alongside KUKU.
Each possesses their own abilities and roles, supporting the harmony of the forest.

Hinoki

Guardian Warrior / The Sentinel

A brave warrior who guards the forest's boundaries. Possessing the strength and fragrance of the hinoki cypress, they support KUKU with unwavering loyalty. Calm and strategic, they stand at the frontlines in times of crisis. Skilled in swordsmanship and defensive magic, they take the greatest pride in protecting their companions.

🖼️ Character Assets

Sakura

Healing Poet / The Healer

An elegant and beautiful healer, graceful as cherry blossoms. Heralding spring's arrival, bringing hope to life. Healing hearts through song and poetry, bringing peace to wounded souls. Possessing delicate sensitivity, empathetically understanding others' pain. Using magic that dances flower petals for healing and purification.

🖼️ Character Assets

Mizuki

Water Sage / The Sage

A mysterious sage governing the waterside. Flexible like flowing water, possessing deep wisdom. Deciphering ancient languages, possessing the power to foresee the future through crystal divination. Gentle by nature, but fierce as a storm when angered. Can freely manipulate water, purifying springs and rivers, summoning rain.

🖼️ Character Assets

Fuji

Dream Weaver / The Dreamer

A mysterious and fantastical being like wisteria flowers. Traversing the boundary between dreams and reality, nurturing hope and imagination. Possessing exceptional artistic sense, creating beautiful scenery in the forest. Manipulating vines to create aerial gardens, showing fantastical visions through illusion magic. Gentle by nature, but inner creativity is infinite.

🖼️ Character Assets

Rino

Wind Messenger / The Messenger

A free-spirited owner of a swift mind like the wind. Running throughout the forest, gathering information and conveying it to companions. Curious and adventurous, loves taking on new challenges. Bright and optimistic personality, the group's mood maker. Can manipulate wind, fly through the sky, and carry distant voices. Detects danger earliest with agility and insight.

🖼️ Character Assets

Sazanka

Flame Guardian / The Keeper

Like the winter-blooming camellia, strong in adversity and passionate. Possessing a warm heart and strong will, devotedly serving companions. Skilled in cooking and fire management, preparing meals for forest companions. Seemingly modest, but inner passion stronger than anyone. Manipulates fire, protects companions from cold, bringing light to darkness. Excels in patience and endurance.
